My Hobbies!

I have had quite a large number of hobbies come and go throughout the past couple years. For example, When covid started to ramp up I decided to invest in a 3D printer. I thought I could use the technology to help people in my community by 3D printing face shields. It would never be a perfect form of protection but it is certainly better than nothing! While I wasn't as succesful as some in this venture, I'm still proud of the things I was able to accomplish.

Currently, I'm playing Guild wars 2. This is a MMORPG set in the world of Tyria. You'll find yourself fighting dragons and other fantastical creatures in this game.
